Service Order Control

A intern Web App to control the service orders (SO) from a Fire Equipaments company.

It turns the work flow more fluid and organized, since all data from costumer registration, SO number, products, cancellation, approval and dates are processed and can be edited on the website itself. Through a QR Code, a microcontroller could tell where the SO are, how much is left to end the service and with processes were already done.

During the development, I learned new languages and new skills, including the usage of a Css framework, PhP em general, modals, Dark Mode and assynchronous requests with Javascript/Jquery.

In this one, was used:
Front-End: html5, css3, Bootstrap, Javascript and Jquery.
Back-End and Databases: PhP, Python and MySQL.
microcontrollers: C/C++.
